lytherin nake

Instructions

Move the Slytherin Snake to steal the golden snitchs, if you hit the wall or your self you lose a life!

You have three lives.

Press S to start.

Press P to Pause.

Press the arrow keys to move up, down, left, right.

Lives: 3

Score: 0

Beat the last high score: 137

GAME OVER!!

Press R to reset the game.
